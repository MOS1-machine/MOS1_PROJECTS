PowerBrick 100W

A high capacity powerbank built around a 4S2P pack of 18650 cells, 3200mAh and 3.7V each, for roughly 26000mAh at 3.7V, or about 96.2Wh total. A 5S capable BMS board with active balancing handles charging and protection, exposing bidirectional USB-C up to 100W with fast charging protocols, micro-USB, two USB-A 3.0 ports and a Lightning port, plus a barrel DC jack. Everything lives inside a custom enclosure modeled in Blender and 3D printed. An XT60 connector wired directly to the 16.8V, 4S pack adds a high current tap, handy for an FPV drone or for powering expansion modules like the lab power supply.
