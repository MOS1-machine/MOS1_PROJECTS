Meshtastic Node

A homemade Meshtastic node built on an ESP32‑S3 paired with a Xiao 868MHz radio module, powered by two 850mAh cells recycled from disposable vapes, with a 128x64 SSD1306 display. Real world range came out to around 1.7 to 2.2km, mostly limited by dense urban buildings. The real win is the price, about 9 euros total.

MCU
ESP32-S3 + Xiao 868MHz
BATTERY
2x 850mAh (recycled vape cells)
DISPLAY
SSD1306 128x64
RANGE
~1.7-2.2 km (urban)
COST
~9 EUR
