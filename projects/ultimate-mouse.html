Ultimate
Mouse

A 5-in-1 custom gaming mouse that switches between mouse, gamepad and HOTAS modes on the fly. Optical sensor, IMU, OLED display, 2.4GHz radio and a bank of macro buttons, all on one board, after seven major firmware iterations.

MCU
ESP32‑S3 Super Mini
SENSOR
PMW3360 (optical)
IMU
MPU‑6500 (direct I2C)
DISPLAY
SSD1306 OLED
RADIO
NRF24L01+PA+LNA
LIGHTING
WS2812B (FastLED)
MODES
GAME / MOUSE / GPAD / HOTAS / HOTAS+
LINK
WIRE + 2.4G (dual mode)
