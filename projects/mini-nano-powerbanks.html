Mini & Nano Powerbanks

Two pocket sized powerbanks sharing the same charging board, with cells recycled from disposable vapes. Nano packs 5 cells at 550mAh each for 2750mAh total, Mini packs 8 cells at 850mAh each for 6800mAh total. Both enclosures were modeled in Blender and FDM printed. Output tops out at 15W on a single USB port or 20W using both.

CELLS
Recycled disposable vape cells
NANO
5 cells, 2750mAh total
MINI
8 cells, 6800mAh total
OUTPUT
15W single USB / 20W dual USB
ENCLOSURE
Blender + FDM print
