Lab Power Supply

A small bench power supply module, 0 to 16V variable output with an adjustable current limiter and short circuit protection. It plugs into the 16.8V, 4S XT60 tap on PowerBrick 100W, turning the powerbank into a portable lab supply for testing and prototyping.

OUTPUT
0-16V, variable
PROTECTION
Adjustable current limit, short circuit
INPUT
16.8V (4S) via XT60
